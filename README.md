📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior using Power BI to identify high-risk segments and revenue exposure.

The dashboard explores:

Contract-based churn patterns

Tenure-based churn concentration

Payment behavior impact

Churn risk scoring (Score ≥ 80)

Revenue at risk segmentation

CLTV impact by churn driver

📈 Key Insights

26.54% overall churn rate

Month-to-Month contracts show highest churn

Customers in first 12 months represent largest churn segment

₹88.55K revenue identified as high-risk (Score ≥ 80)

Pricing and onboarding experience significantly influence early churn

1️⃣ Executive Overview

High-level KPIs, churn segmentation by contract, payment method, and senior citizens.

2️⃣ Customer Behavior Analysis

Top churn drivers, CLTV impact, and revenue at risk visualization.
